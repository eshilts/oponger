Rulez

League play will be ongoing, with ranks updated on a game-to-game basis. Games can be played in WATT (i.e. Conference Room 2) and wherever the San Francisco folks figure out a spot to play (TBD).

League games should preferably be played as best of 5 (or best of 3 when in a rush or others are waiting to play) to 11 points. This is a bit different from the traditional OPOWER 21, but give it a try. You might find the games to move more quickly and be a bit more exciting. Make sure to switch sides after each game to mitigate any one side's effect on the outcome. For more details on the rules of play see this handy reference

The eight top ranked players will compete in a single-elimination tournament on Cinco De Mustache for the 2011 OPONGER title. Contenders for the title will play for best 3/5 games.

Beyond that, rules are for whiners and cheats. If you have questions or need a ref for something, talk to Sean Owen (a.k.a. sean@sucks.com).

Rankings

Rankings are calculated using the ELO rating system.
